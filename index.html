About Me

Hi, I'm Muhammad Tahir, a multidisciplinary creative professional specializing as a Graphic Designer, UI/UX Designer, Brand Identity Specialist, and Video Editor.

With over 1.5 years of industry experience, I have partnered with dynamic organizations like One Atom Solutions, Clan Group, Xpertsones, and XpertDigi to craft functional and visually striking digital experiences.

My hands-on expertise is backed by a solid academic foundation, including a BS in Visual Communication from the University of Management and Technology (UMT), specialized 6-month diplomas in both UI/UX and Graphic Design from PNY, and a 2-month Video Editing certification from NAVTTC.

Whether I am building intuitive user interfaces, shaping brand identities, or editing impactful video content, my goal is always to translate complex ideas into seamless, modern designs that help brands stand out.
